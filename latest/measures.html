    TextStylometry.dugast_k — Function.
    dugast_k(N)

    Dugast (1979) (T&B Eq. 6)

    TextStylometry.a² — Function.
    a²(N)

    TextStylometry.dugast_U — Function.
    dugast_U(N)

    TextStylometry.LN — Function.
    LN(N)

    Lukjanenkov & Nesitoj (1975; in Tulvada, 1977) (Eq. 9)

    \[V(N) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 + LN \log N}}\]
    source
    TextStylometry.brunet_W — Function.
    brunet_W(N)

    Brunet's parametric expression W (1978) (Eq. 10)

    \[W = N^{V(N)^{-a}},\]

    where a = -0.172 and

    \[V(N) = (\frac{\log W}{\log N})^a = \log^a_N W.\]
    source
